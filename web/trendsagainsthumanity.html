Trends Against Humanity

What is this? Trends Against Humanity is an experiment in blending Cards Against Humanity with Twitter.

What is Cards Against Humanity? Cards Against Humanity is “a party game for horrible people”. It's really fun and you should check it out.

What is Twitter? Twitter is a service where you can subscribe to 140-character phrases generated by a whole slew of bots, which some pundits now refer to as the fall of humanity. You should check it out too.

How does it work? We select a Cards Against Humanity black card to be the question, and then uses Twitter trending topics as a standin for the white cards in order to answer the question. Much like the original game, the results can be spectacular.

Can I get regularly-scheduled deliveries? Every two hours @trendagainst will tweet a new combination.
